INTRODUCTION

Ryan Sports Club is a prominent sports organization dedicated to promoting physical fitness, sportsmanship, and athletic development among individuals of all ages. Established with a vision to foster a culture of health, wellness, and teamwork, the club offers a wide range of sports programs and activities for its members. From training sessions in popular sports like football, basketball, cricket, and athletics, to providing state-of-the-art facilities, Ryan Sports Club is committed to nurturing talent and encouraging community participation in sport456 The club aims to create a platform for both amateur and professional athletes to excel and develop their skills, while also promoting a strong sense of discipline, leadership, and collaboration. Through its various training programs, events, and competitions, Ryan Sports Club strives to build not only athletic excellence but also strong social bonds within the community.


To become a member of the Ryan Sports Club, simply visit our website or visit our main office. Fill out the membership application form, pay the required fees, and you will receive your membership ID. You can choose from various membership packages based on the type of access and services you prefer.

Individual Membership: For single members who wish to access facilities and participate in our events. Family Membership: Includes access for two adults and children under 18 years. Premium Membership: Includes priority booking for facilities, exclusive events, and special discounts. Corporate Membership: For businesses that want to offer their employees access to the club.

Sports facilities such as football, cricket, basketball courts, and swimming pools. Gym and fitness center with expert trainers. Yoga and wellness programs. Locker rooms and changing facilities. Access to our regular workshops and events.

The cost of membership varies depending on the type of membership you choose. For individual members, prices start at $50 per month, while family and corporate memberships offer discounts for multiple members. Please contact our office or visit our website for detailed pricing.
