Past Events of Basketball

Relive the excitement of our past events!

Basketball Event- 2024

Date: August 13, 2024 | Location: RYAN Sports Club

The 2024 Basketball Tournament took place from August 13-15 at RYAN Sports Club, featuring thrilling competitions . The Downtown Ballers defended their title in a dramatic 92-87 win over the River City Titans, while the Golden Eagles triumphed 75-68 against the Lakeside Lions in the final. The event also featured standout performances, including Tasha Green’s MVP performance and Tori Allen’s record-breaking 9 three-pointers. Fans were treated to intense action, community spirit, and exciting side events like a Celebrity Dunk Contest.

Basketball Event- 2023

Date: June 16, 2023 | Location:RYAN Sports Club

The 2023 Men’s Basketball Tournament was a massive success, drawing together teams from all over the region for an unforgettable weekend of competition, camaraderie, and high-flying action. Held at RYAN Sports Club in Karachi, the event showcased some of the best talent in the area, delivering thrilling matchups from start to finish.

National Basketball Event For Women - 2022

Date: July 21, 2022 | Location:RYAN Sports Club

The 2022 Women's Basketball Match was held on July at RYAN Sports Club. The Golden Eagles clinched the championship with a 75-68 victory over the Lakeside Lions, with Tasha Green named MVP. The event featured standout performances, including Tori Allen's record-breaking 9 three-pointers in a single game. Fans were treated to intense competition and a celebration of local basketball talent.
